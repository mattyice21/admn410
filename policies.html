Financing Policies

No lay-away. All transactions must be paid in full, in cluding tax and shipping fees.

Delivery Policies

Free Delivery if customer spends $100 or more on products. Shipping accounts only for business days. If products are damaged during shipping and handling, we will issue a refund or resend the order with undamaged product.

Return Policies

14 days for return, no exceptions for extensions. Returns via shipping and in person are accepted as long as they are within the 14 day range
